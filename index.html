Broad Arrow Group represents the collective goal of its partners and employees to be the best advisor marketplace and financier serving car collectors globally, with utmost integrity.

our Story

C

ar collectors and enthusiasts globally buy and sell $25 billion in collector cars each year. We set out to create a company that changes the ways in which they do so.

Broad Arrow Group (BAG) was founded to transform the global marketplace for collector cars. We are a group of industry veterans who not only share a passion for cars but also the goal of bringing a new level of simplicity, transparency and accountability to transacting collector cars the world over across different channels and platforms, and in doing so, delivering a more enjoyable experience for the enthusiast.

The collector car industry is highly fragmented, with varying degrees of honesty and business integrity among industry participants, alongside extraordinary amounts of information and misinformation. With hundreds of dealers, dozens of auction houses and tens of thousands of cars offered for sale, how do you know who and what to trust?

Broad Arrow Group addresses this need with industry-leading experience and expertise. Regardless of how you choose to buy or sell your collector car(s), BAG ensures the experience is simple, straightforward and honest.
